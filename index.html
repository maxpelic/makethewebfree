make the web free

Yeah, browsing the web is free (assuming you have an internet connection). But shouldn't developing for it be free as well? Let's give everyone the ability to create amazing websites without paying a cent.

Our plan is to create a library of free resources available to anyone who wants them - no strings attached. Yeah, people can pay us if they want to support an awesome library, but they won't get any special benefits from doing so (other than knowing the contributed to an amazing set of resources)

Some project ideas we have so far: IP address location lookup (used to check compliance with local laws), finishing development of this site (obviously), and a free privacy policy generator moderated by the community.

How can you show you're support? Right now we're in the concept/development phase of this, but if you're interested in getting involved, we'd love to hear from you. You can email connect@makethewebfree.com with any thoughts, requests, or ideas you have.

Thanks,

- MTWF team, March 27th, 2019
